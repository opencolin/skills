# Content Engine Builder

Turn one strong source idea into multiple useful content assets and a repeatable publishing loop.

## Workflow

1. Identify the source thesis, audience, pain, stakes, proof, and point of view.
2. Extract reusable idea atoms:
   - contrarian belief
   - client pain
   - lesson
   - framework
   - story
   - proof point
   - CTA
3. Choose content lanes that fit the business: education, proof, belief, behind-the-scenes, case study, diagnostic, or direct offer.
4. Generate assets in different formats while preserving one coherent voice.
5. Add distribution notes: platform, format, CTA, and follow-up prompt.
6. Suggest the next source artifact to create.

```markdown
## Publishing Plan
| Asset | Platform | CTA | Follow-Up |

<recommendation>
```

## Quality Bar

- Do not flatten the user's point of view into generic advice.
- Preserve useful rough edges when they make the voice memorable.
- Make every asset point somewhere: conversation, reply, signup, call, or sale.
- Avoid fake virality claims.
