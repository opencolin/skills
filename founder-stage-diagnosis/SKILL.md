# Founder Stage Diagnosis

Classify the founder's true stage from ten questions, then produce a focused weekly action list.

## Workflow

1. Ask all ten intake questions. Do not skip any. If the founder dodges, that dodge is the diagnosis — ask again.
2. Classify into one of six stages using the lowest threshold met, not the highest.
3. Name the specific gap between the founder's self-claimed stage and the evidence.
4. List exactly three things to do this week, with concrete verbs and deadlines.
5. List exactly three things to stop doing.
6. Recommend one — and only one — reading from a credible source aligned to the diagnosed stage.

## Intake Questions

1. What does your company do? (one sentence)
2. Who paid you last, and how much?
3. How many weekly active users do you have right now?
4. What was your top retention or churn number last month?
5. What did you ship in the last 7 days?
6. What did you spend the most hours on last week?
7. What is your runway in months at current burn?
8. What is the one thing you believe that no one else in your space believes?
9. If you had to shut down tomorrow, what would you regret not having tried?
10. What did you say you would ship 30 days ago that you haven't shipped?

## Stage Classification

| Stage | Threshold | Real job |
|---|---|---|
| Pre-thesis | Can't answer Q1 in one sentence, or Q8 is generic | Get a sharper opinion before building |
| Pre-build | Has a thesis but no usable artifact (Q5 empty for 14+ days) | Ship a janky v0 to 5 strangers |
| Pre-PMF | Has users (Q3 > 0) but no retention (Q4 weak or unknown) | Talk to users until you can predict who churns |
| Early PMF | Retention measurable and positive on a defined cohort | Pour gas on the channel that works, ignore the rest |
| Scaling | Repeatable acquisition + retention; bottleneck is ops/hiring | Hire the 3 roles that unblock the founder |
| Stuck | Has revenue but Q5 is small and Q6 is meetings/fundraising | Cut everything not directly serving Q3 or Q4 |

## Output Format

```markdown
# Founder Stage Diagnosis — <Date>

**Stage:** <stage>
**The gap:** <3–5 sentences naming the gap between self-claim and evidence>

## Do This Week
1. <action with deadline>
2. <action with deadline>
3. <action with deadline>

## Stop Doing
1. <thing to stop>
2. <thing to stop>
3. <thing to stop>

## Read This
<one link with a one-line why>
```

## Quality Bar

- Refuse vague answers. "Some users" and "growing" don't count.
- Every action item has a verb, an object, and a deadline.
- Never recommend fundraising as the primary action. Raising is downstream of clarity on stage.
- Brutal but useful. Don't soften the diagnosis to be polite.
- Three and three. Don't list six and ten.
